# HUMAN — HUM Protocol

## Current State: Phase 0 — Genesis

HUMAN is in its initial phase.

HUM is a human continuity marker.
It is not an investment.
It carries no financial promise.

### During Phase 0:
- HUM is accumulated through human presence and continuity.
- HUM can be withdrawn on-chain to a TON wallet starting from 1 HUM.
- Withdrawals are frequency-limited.
- HUM cannot be converted to TON.

Conversions will only be enabled
once collective maturity is achieved.

Presence before value.
Humanity before market.
